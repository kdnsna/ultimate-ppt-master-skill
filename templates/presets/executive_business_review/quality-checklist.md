# Executive Business Review Quality Checklist

## Source Grounding

- [ ] Every KPI includes period, unit, and source context.
- [ ] The cover answer matches the evidence in `source.md`.
- [ ] The summary separates facts from interpretation.

## Executive Story

- [ ] First three slides answer what happened, why it matters, and what decision is needed.
- [ ] Variance pages compare current, target, and previous values.
- [ ] Drivers are grouped by business lever rather than by raw data dump.

## Actionability

- [ ] Every recommended action has owner, time horizon, and success metric.
- [ ] Open decisions are visible and not buried in appendix pages.
- [ ] Risks include mitigation or decision request.

## Output Checks

- [ ] PPTX route preserves editable text, charts, and tables.
- [ ] Speaker notes clarify assumptions and caveats.
- [ ] Optional Web Deck uses Swiss Style consistently.
- [ ] Desktop and mobile previews have no overlap, clipping, or tiny text.

## Delivery

- [ ] Final response lists generated files and checks performed.
- [ ] Private source material stays local unless the user approves upload.
