# Consulting Proposal Source Skeleton

## Project

Client growth diagnosis and implementation recommendation.

## Audience

Client executives, project sponsor, and steering committee.

## Decision Question

Which operating route should the client choose for the next two quarters, and what must change in organization, process, and measurement to make it work?

## Current Situation

- Growth is positive but uneven across customer segments and channels.
- The client has several improvement ideas, but no shared prioritization logic.
- The steering committee needs a recommendation that can become a funded implementation plan.

## Evidence To Provide

- KPI table by segment, channel, or product.
- Customer journey pain points or interview notes.
- Process bottlenecks and ownership gaps.
- Current resource constraints.
- Known technology or compliance constraints.
- Decision deadline and governance cadence.

## Option Set

1. Optimize current process with targeted quick wins.
2. Rebuild the customer journey around high-value segments.
3. Launch a dedicated cross-functional growth program.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Expected business impact.
- Implementation speed.
- Cost and resource load.
- Operational risk.
- Ease of governance.

## Required Output

- Primary: editable PPTX for client review.
- Optional: Swiss-style Web Deck for an internal steering meeting.

## Notes For The Agent

- Avoid generic consulting language unless it is supported by source evidence.
- Make the recommendation explicit by slide 2.
- Use issue tree, option matrix, roadmap, and risk/governance pages.
- Keep assumptions visible.
