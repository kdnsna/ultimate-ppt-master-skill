# Consulting Proposal Quality Checklist

## Story

- [ ] Slide 1 or 2 states the decision question and recommended route.
- [ ] The diagnosis distinguishes symptoms, root causes, and constraints.
- [ ] The option comparison uses named evaluation criteria.
- [ ] The recommendation is traceable to the criteria and evidence.
- [ ] The roadmap has phases, milestones, owners, and dependencies.

## Evidence

- [ ] Every major claim has source support or is labeled as an assumption.
- [ ] Data tables include period, unit, and source.
- [ ] Risks are not hidden in appendix-only pages.
- [ ] Commercial or resourcing estimates are marked as directional unless sourced.

## Design And Output

- [ ] PPTX text, shapes, charts, and speaker notes remain editable.
- [ ] Dense consulting pages still have a single primary takeaway.
- [ ] Tables and charts are readable at 16:9 presentation size.
- [ ] Final files include the source skeleton, exported deck, and any preview artifacts.
