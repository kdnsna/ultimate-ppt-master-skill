# Government / SOE Report Direction

Use for government, SOE, policy, infrastructure, public-service, and formal work reports.

## Visual Standard

- Authoritative, conservative, policy-context first.
- Use strong title hierarchy, restrained red/blue/gold accents, and structured evidence.
- Favor workstream tables, timelines, responsibility maps, and risk-control pages.

## Required Page Roles

- `anchor`, `context`, `policy`, `evidence`, `workstream`, `timeline`, `risk`, `closing`.

## Asset Policy

- Official marks only, or visible fallback status.
- Real project/site/policy material preferred; placeholders must be labeled.

## Anti-Patterns

- Startup pitch visual language.
- Unverified “first/only/best” claims.
- Thin pages with one vague slogan and no evidence.
- Oversized decorative icons replacing actual proof.
