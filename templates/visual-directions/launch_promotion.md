# Launch / Promotion Direction

Use for product launches, campaigns, cultural tourism, events, venue promotion, and external storytelling decks.

## Visual Standard

- Visual-first and asset-led.
- First screen must show the product, place, person, gameplay, scene, or actual state.
- Use full-bleed imagery, hero typography, and short copy.

## Required Page Roles

- `anchor`, `hero`, `scene`, `proof`, `offer`, `journey`, `action`, `closing`.

## Asset Policy

- Real or generated bitmap visuals are mandatory.
- Pure gradients, SVG illustrations, and stock-like atmosphere are insufficient when the audience needs to inspect the real object/place/state.

## Anti-Patterns

- Text-only promotional decks.
- Formal report tables as the dominant layout.
- Generic decorative background with no subject signal.
- Cropped/blurred assets that hide the thing being promoted.
