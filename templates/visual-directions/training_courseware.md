# Training Courseware Direction

Use for internal training, onboarding, workshops, and teaching decks.

## Visual Standard

- Modular, explicit, and learner-action oriented.
- Each section should have objective, concept, example, practice, and summary.
- Use diagrams, process visuals, examples, and checklists.

## Required Page Roles

- `anchor`, `agenda`, `objective`, `concept`, `example`, `practice`, `checklist`, `closing`.

## Asset Policy

- Examples and screenshots are more valuable than decoration.
- Speaker notes should support delivery.

## Anti-Patterns

- Long theory pages without examples.
- No learner task or practice step.
- Random icons used as decoration.
- Dense text copied directly from source without chunking.
