# Finance Internal Report Direction

Use for banking, insurance, wealth, social-card, branch operation, and internal stakeholder decks.

## Visual Standard

- Formal, restrained, evidence-first.
- White or very light report pages with strong hierarchy.
- Brand color is structural: cover, section markers, rules, footer, key numbers.
- Red/accent color is limited to risk, conclusion, or action emphasis.
- Dense pages are allowed, but density must be organized through tables, timelines, sidebars, or chart-led layouts.

## Required Page Roles

- `anchor`: cover, chapter, tail.
- `context`: why this topic matters.
- `evidence`: progress, data, city/branch list, proof.
- `process`: service path, operating flow, customer journey.
- `risk`: conditions, boundaries, FAQ, compliance caveats.
- `benefit`: rights, incentives, customer value.

## Asset Policy

- Real logo or documented text fallback.
- Official screenshots, card samples, process screenshots, branch/field photos, or clearly labeled schematics.
- No fake official assets.

## Anti-Patterns

- Internet SaaS cards with large rounded panels on every page.
- Decorative gradients, floating blobs, emoji, fake logo letters.
- No source matrix for fact-heavy claims.
- Every content page using the same 2x2 card grid.
