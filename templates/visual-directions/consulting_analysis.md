# Consulting Analysis Direction

Use for executive analysis, business review, diagnosis, recommendations, and strategic options.

## Visual Standard

- Conclusion-first and chart-led.
- Every page has a governing sentence.
- Charts and tables carry the proof; sidebars interpret.
- Whitespace is used to expose decision logic, not to look decorative.

## Required Page Roles

- `anchor`, `tldr`, `evidence`, `diagnosis`, `comparison`, `options`, `roadmap`, `risk`, `closing`.

## Asset Policy

- Data visualizations, tables, roadmaps, issue trees, and decision matrices count as primary visuals.
- Photos are optional unless they prove product/place/state.

## Anti-Patterns

- Generic feature cards without decision criteria.
- Recommendation not supported by evidence.
- Chart with no takeaway.
- Repeating the same card layout for all analysis pages.
