# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Ultimate PPT Master to be a welcoming project for people improving AI-assisted presentation workflows. Be respectful, practical, and generous with context.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and tool preferences.
- Keep feedback specific, constructive, and focused on the work.
- Redact private business material before sharing examples.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private documents, secrets, or personal data.
- Repeatedly derailing issues, discussions, or pull requests.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its users. To report a concern, open a private channel with the maintainer or use the security contact listed in `SECURITY.md` when private information is involved.
